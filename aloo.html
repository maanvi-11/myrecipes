Instructions:

Prepare Dough: Mix wheat flour, salt, and water to make soft dough. Rest 15 mins. Make Filling: Mix mashed potatoes with chilies, ginger, cumin, salt, and spices. Stuff & Roll: Roll small dough ball, add filling, seal, and gently roll again. Cook: Roast on hot tawa with ghee/oil until golden on both sides. Serve hot with curd, butter, or pickle!
