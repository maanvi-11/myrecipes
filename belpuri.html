Recipe:

Ingredients

2 cups puffed rice (murmura) 1/2 cup chopped onions 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes 1/4 cup boiled potatoes (cubed) 2 tbsp green chutney 2 tbsp tamarind chutney 2 tbsp sev (for topping) 1 tbsp chopped coriander Salt & chaat masala to taste Lemon juice (to taste)

Instructions:

Mix: In a large bowl, mix puffed rice, onions, tomatoes, potatoes, and chutneys. Season: Add salt, chaat masala, lemon juice, and coriander. Toss quickly. Serve: Top with sev and serve immediately (before it turns soggy).
