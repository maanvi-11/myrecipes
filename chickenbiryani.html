Recipe

Ingredients

500g chicken (with bone) 1.5 cups basmati rice 1 cup curd 2 onions (sliced) 2 tomatoes (chopped) 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom) Coriander & mint leaves Oil/ghee, salt to taste

Instructions:

Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with curd, spices, ginger-garlic paste. Marinate for 30 mins. Cook Rice: Boil basmati rice with salt and whole spices till 80% done. Drain. Prepare Masala: Fry onions till golden. Add tomatoes, masala, and marinated chicken. Cook till chicken is tender. Layer & Dum: In a pot, layer chicken masala and rice. Top with fried onions, coriander, mint. Cover and cook on low (dum) for 15â€“20 mins. Serve hot with raita or salan.
