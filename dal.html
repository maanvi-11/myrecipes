Recipe:

Ingredients

For Dal: 1/2 cup toor dal (or moong dal) 1/4 tsp turmeric Salt to taste 2 cups water

Tempering:

1 tbsp ghee/oil 1/2 tsp mustard or cumin seeds 1 garlic clove (chopped) 1 dry red chili A pinch of hing Curry leaves (optional) For Chawal (Rice): 1 cup rice 2 cups water Salt to taste

Instructions:

Cook Dal: Pressure cook dal with turmeric and salt until soft. Mash slightly. Add Tempering: Heat ghee, add tempering ingredients. Pour over dal. Cook Rice: Boil rice with water and salt until fluffy. Serve: Serve dal hot with steamed rice and ghee.
