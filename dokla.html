Recipe:

Ingredients

Ingredients: 1 cup besan (gram flour) 1/2 cup water 1 tbsp lemon juice 1 tsp Eno fruit salt (or 1/2 tsp baking soda) 1 tsp sugar 1/2 tsp turmeric Salt to taste For Tempering: 1 tbsp oil 1/2 tsp mustard seeds 5–6 curry leaves 2 chopped green chilies 2 tbsp water 1 tsp sugar

Instructions:

Batter: Mix besan, turmeric, salt, sugar, lemon juice, and water to make a smooth batter. Add Eno, stir gently. Batter will become fluffy. Steam: Pour batter into a greased plate. Steam for 15–20 mins. Temper: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, water, and sugar. Boil for 1 min. Serve: Pour tempering on dhokla. Cut into squares and serve with green chutney.
