Recipe:

Ingredients

1 cup urad dal (split black gram) 3 cups rice (preferably dosa rice or parboiled rice) 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds Salt to taste Water as needed Oil for cooking

Instructions:

Soak: Soak urad dal + fenugreek and rice separately for 4â€“6 hours. Grind: Grind dal to a fluffy batter and rice to a smooth batter. Mix both. Add salt and let it ferment overnight. Cook: Heat a non-stick pan or tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread in a circle. Drizzle oil and cook until crisp and golden. Serve: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
