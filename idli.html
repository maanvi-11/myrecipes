Recipe

Ingredients:

1 cup urad dal (split black gram) 2 cups idli rice Salt to taste Water (as needed)

Instructions:

Soak: Soak urad dal and idli rice separately in water for 4–6 hours. Grind: Grind urad dal to a smooth batter. Grind rice coarsely. Mix both batters. Ferment: Add salt, mix well, and ferment overnight (8–12 hrs) until batter rises. Steam: Pour batter into greased idli molds. Steam for 10–12 minutes.
