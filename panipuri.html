Recipe:

Ingredients

Puris (or use ready-made): 1/2 cup semolina (sooji) 2 tbsp maida (optional) Water & salt to knead

For Filling:

1 cup boiled, mashed potatoes 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas or moong Salt, chaat masala to taste For Spicy Water (Pani): 1 cup mint leaves 1/2 cup coriander 1–2 green chilies 1 tsp roasted cumin powder 1/2 tsp black salt Tamarind pulp (1–2 tbsp) Water (2–3 cups)

Instructions:

Make Pani: Blend mint, coriander, chilies, tamarind, and spices with water. Chill. Prepare Filling: Mix mashed potatoes, chickpeas, and spices. Assemble: Crack puris, add filling, pour spicy pani, and serve immediately.
