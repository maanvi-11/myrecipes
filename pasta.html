Recipe:

Ingredients

1 cup pasta (any type) 1 tbsp oil 1/2 onion (chopped) 1/2 capsicum (chopped) 1 tomato or 2 tbsp tomato sauce 1/2 tsp chili flakes & oregano Salt to taste Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

Boil Pasta: Cook pasta in salted water. Drain and keep aside. Sauté Veggies: Heat oil, sauté onion and capsicum. Add tomato/sauce and cook. Mix Pasta: Add cooked pasta, salt, chili flakes, and oregano. Toss well. Serve: Top with cheese if desired. Serve hot!
