Recipe

Ingredients

For Base: 1 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 tsp yeast or baking powder (for instant) Salt & water to knead 1 tbsp oil For Topping: 1/4 cup pizza sauce Grated mozzarella cheese Veggies: onion, capsicum, tomato, corn, etc. Oregano, chili flakes

Instructions:

Make Dough: Mix flour, yeast/baking powder, salt, water, and oil. Rest 30 mins (skip if using ready base). Assemble: Roll dough, spread sauce, add cheese and veggies. Sprinkle herbs. Bake: Bake at 200°C (392°F) for 12–15 minutes or until crust is golden. Serve hot and cheesy!
