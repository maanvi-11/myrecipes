Recipe:

Ingredients

cup poha (flattened rice) 1 small onion (chopped) 1 green chili (chopped) 1/4 tsp mustard seeds 5–6 curry leaves 1/4 tsp turmeric 1 tbsp peanuts Salt to taste 1 tsp lemon juice Fresh coriander (for garnish) 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

Rinse Poha: Rinse poha in water, drain, and keep aside for 5–10 minutes. Temper: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, peanuts, curry leaves, and green chili. Sauté chopped onions until soft. Mix: Add turmeric, salt, and soaked poha. Mix well. Finish: Cook for 2–3 minutes.
