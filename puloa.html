Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredients: 1 cup basmati rice 1.5–2 cups mixed veggies (carrot, beans, peas, etc.) 1 onion (sliced) 1–2 green chilies Whole spices (1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 cardamoms) 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste 2 tbsp oil or ghee Salt to taste 2 cups water

Instructions:

Sauté: Heat oil, add whole spices, then onions and chilies. Sauté. Add Veggies: Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped veggies. Cook 2–3 mins. Add Rice: Add soaked rice, salt, and water. Mix and bring to boil. Cook: Cover and cook on low flame till rice is done and fluffy. Serve hot with raita or salad!
