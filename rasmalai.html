Recipe:

Instrution

For Rasgullas: 1 liter milk 2 tbsp lemon juice 1 cup sugar 4 cups water For Rabri (Milk Syrup): 1/2 liter milk 2–3 tbsp sugar A few saffron strands 1/4 tsp cardamom powder Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)

Instructions:

Make Chenna: Boil milk, add lemon juice, strain and rinse the curdled milk. Knead into soft dough and shape small balls. Boil Rasgullas: Cook balls in boiling sugar water for 12–15 mins. Let cool. Prepare Rabri: Boil milk till reduced. Add sugar, saffron, cardamom, and nuts. Assemble: Squeeze rasgullas gently and soak them in chilled rabri. Serve chilled for best taste!
