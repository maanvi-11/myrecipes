Recipe:

Ingredients

1/2 cup toor dal 1/2 cup rice 1 onion & 1 tomato (chopped) Mixed veggies (carrot, beans, drumstick, etc.) 2 tbsp sambar powder 1/4 tsp turmeric Salt to taste Tamarind (small lemon-sized ball) 2 cups water

Tempering:

1 tbsp ghee or oil 1/2 tsp mustard seeds 1 dry red chili A few curry leaves A pinch of asafoetida (hing)

Instructions:

Cook: Pressure cook rice and dal with turmeric and veggies. Mix: Add tamarind juice, sambar powder, and salt. Simmer for 5â€“10 mins. Temper: Heat ghee, add mustard, red chili, curry leaves, and hing. Pour over sambar rice. Serve: Mix well and serve hot with papad or pickle.
